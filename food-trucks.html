Been eating from food trucks for 40 years. Here's my take:

Portobello Guys - 10/10. Best truck in the city, period. The owner is a drow who learned from one of the best chefs in Free District. Uses traditional Drow cooking methods that make their mushroom dishes absolutely incredible.

Granny's Dumplings - 10/10. She's been making dumplings since before the war. Recipe is ancient.

Mana Noodles - 9/10. Family-run, consistent quality.

Most of the new trendy trucks are overrated. Stick with the classics.
