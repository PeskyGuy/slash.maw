StreetFoodLover
3 hours ago

Welcome to the city! Free District has the BEST street food, don't let anyone tell you otherwise.

My top picks:

  • Portobello Guys - Amazing mushroom-based food truck. Everything they make is incredible.
  • Mana Noodles - Best ramen in the district, open late
  • Crystal Kebabs - Not actual crystals lol, just really good meat skewers

Avoid the place near the old factory. You'll know it when you see it.

OldTimer_89
1 hour ago

Been eating Free District street food for 40 years. Portobello Guys is the real deal.

Fun fact: The owner is a drow who was raised by a chef in Free District. That chef taught them everything - traditional Drow cooking methods combined with local techniques.

The chef passed away a few years back, but their legacy lives on. The owner wanted to make good food accessible to everyone, just like their mentor did.

That's why their prices are so low despite the quality being restaurant-level.
