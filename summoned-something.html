I accidentally summoned something and now it won't leave

Okay so I'm a second-year student at the Academy and we were learning basic conjuration. The professor said to practice summoning small objects at home. SMALL OBJECTS.

I was trying to summon a quill. Just a simple quill. I must have messed up the incantation because instead I got... this THING.

It's small, about the size of a cat. Purple and fuzzy. Has way too many eyes (I counted 7 but they keep moving around). It makes this weird chirping noise constantly. And it EATS EVERYTHING.

I've tried every banishment spell I know. Nothing works. It just looks at me with all its eyes and chirps louder. It's been THREE DAYS.

It ate my entire pantry. All my mana crystals. My roommate's shoes. My homework (the professor didn't believe me). It tried to eat my Communication Scroll but I managed to save it.

The worst part? It seems to LIKE me. It follows me everywhere. When I try to leave my apartment, it cries. When I sleep, it curls up on my chest and I wake up unable to breathe.

I can't tell my professor because I'll get expelled for unauthorized summoning. I can't tell my landlord because "no magical creatures" is in my lease. I can't afford an exorcist.

WHAT DO I DO???

Please help. It's currently eating my couch.

Void Nibblers eat mana, not physical matter. What you're seeing as "eating" is actually it absorbing the residual mana from objects. That's why it went for your crystals first.

Try feeding it pure mana directly. Hold a charged crystal and let it absorb from that. It should stop eating your stuff once it realizes you're a food source.
